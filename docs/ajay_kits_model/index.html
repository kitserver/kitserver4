Kit Models

Here is a complete list of the default kit models along with their descriptions. I have also included the team(s) that tends to use that kit model. I am in the process of adding graphical previews to distinguish the differences where applicable, this part is still work in progress and will be completed soon. The kit models will be particularly useful when you need to modify a kit in kitserver. You may need to tweak the location of the shirt number, style or name and the table below gives you a good guide as to which model you may need to use for that particular kit. Special thanks go to Waterloo, it was via his superb utility I was able to study and decipher the kit models.
